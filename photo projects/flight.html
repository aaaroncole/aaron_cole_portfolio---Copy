UA 1095

in flight • march 9, 2025

I took these on an early morining flight from DC to San Francisco - my connecting flight to Baja. Travelling east to west, we ran away from the sun, making the flight a 4.5 hour sunrise across the country.

Some of the most interesting forms I saw were in Great Salt Lake. The roads creating constrasts in the water that meet the mountains are something you could only fully appreciate from the air. I hope to see it from the ground someday.
