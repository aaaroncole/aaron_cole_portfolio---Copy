UA 1095

in flight • march 9, 2025

A collection capturing the spontaneous beauty found in everyday street life across Brooklyn neighborhoods. These images explore the intersection of light, movement, and human connection in urban spaces.

Shot over several months during different seasons, the series documents how the same streets transform with changing weather, time of day, and the rhythms of city life. Each photograph seeks to find poetry in the ordinary moments that often go unnoticed.
