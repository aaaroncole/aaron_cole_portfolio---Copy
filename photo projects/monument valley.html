Tsé Biiʼ Ndzisgaii

navajo nation • summer 2025

We arrived at Monument Valley just as the sun started to set. We were two of many sitting at the View Hotel. Locals, tourists, dogs, and horses all gathered together for the event - the everydayness of the event seemed to have no effect on the locals' appreciation for the moment. Their connection to their land is something we should strive for.
