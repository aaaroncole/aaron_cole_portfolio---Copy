DUNE

great sand dunes national park • 2022-present

An ongoing study of the dunes in this park and their everchanging forms. I've captured these photos over the course of multiple trips I've taken over the past few years.

Most of these were taken during a camping trip I took with a friend in the summer of 2024. During our hike the weather was chaotic - rain, wind, sand pelting our legs, lightning accompanied by static noise and our hair standing up. By morning, the most beautifully still sunrise I've ever seen.
