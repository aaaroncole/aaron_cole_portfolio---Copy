Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Aaron Cole and I'm a 5th year student at Syracuse University pursuing a B.Arch with a minor in mathematics. Here you can find all my architecture and photography projects that I've worked on in and out of school.

I was born into an Irish-American family in Galway, Ireland, and moved to St. Louis, Missouri at a very young age. I've always felt that growing up in an immigrant family in the heart of the midwest gave me a unique perspective. This combined with my study abroad semester in Italy and an educational trip to Mexico City have exposed me to a wide range of different views on architecture and life.

I've worked on a variety of projects including museums, housing, installations, and a memorial. Through my architectural and photography work I aim to bring awareness to our surroundings, harboring space for a newly found appreciation of our world. I am currently researching the origins and spread of suburbia before and throughout the United States. This research will culminate in a design project - akin to a thesis - which will be complete in Spring 2026.

Outside of architecture and photography I have studied and performed classical piano music since I was five years old. I participated in many competitions throughout the years, and performed a thesis in 2021. Recently, I've been teaching myself how to produce music in FL Studio. My music education and practice will continue to be an integral part of my development as an architect, artist, and person.
