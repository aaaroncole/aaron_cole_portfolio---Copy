Spiral Installation

Installation Architecture · Location, 2023

Project Overview

The Spiral Installation explores the relationship between movement, perception, and architectural form through a carefully choreographed spatial sequence. This temporary intervention creates a contemplative journey that unfolds gradually, revealing different perspectives and experiences as visitors navigate the spiraling path.

Drawing inspiration from both natural and mathematical spirals, the installation questions how architectural form can guide human movement and create moments of discovery. The design emphasizes the phenomenological aspects of space, where the experience of walking becomes as important as the destination itself.

Constructed using sustainable materials and designed for disassembly, the installation demonstrates how temporary architecture can create profound spatial experiences without permanent environmental impact. The spiral form serves both as organizing principle and metaphor for cycles of construction, inhabitation, and renewal.

This project investigates how minimal interventions can transform understanding of site and context, creating new relationships between body, movement, and architectural space through the simple yet powerful geometry of the spiral.
