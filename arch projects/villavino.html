Villa Vino

Residential Architecture · Tuscany, Italy, 2023

Project Overview

Villa Vino represents a contemporary interpretation of traditional Tuscan architecture, seamlessly blending modern living requirements with the timeless character of the Italian countryside. Located among rolling hills and established vineyards, the villa respects its agricultural context while providing sophisticated spaces for both intimate family life and entertaining.

The design organizes the program around a central courtyard, a typology deeply rooted in Mediterranean architecture. This arrangement creates natural ventilation, frames views of the surrounding landscape, and establishes a hierarchy of public and private spaces that flow from interior to exterior with fluid transitions.

Material choices emphasize local stone, reclaimed timber, and handcrafted details that connect the villa to regional building traditions. Contemporary interventions are integrated subtly, with modern amenities and technologies embedded within the traditional framework to ensure comfort without compromising authenticity.

The villa's relationship to the working vineyard is central to the design concept, with wine production and storage facilities integrated into the lower level, creating a true agricultural residence that celebrates both the craft of winemaking and the beauty of rural Italian life.
